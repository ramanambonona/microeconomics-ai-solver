Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

MicroSolver AI is designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect or store any personal information. The application runs entirely in your browser, and we do not have servers that store user data.

2. API Keys

Your API keys for DeepSeek or OpenAI are stored locally in your browser and are never transmitted to our servers. They are only used to make direct requests to the respective AI APIs.

3. Problem Data

The microeconomics problems you input are sent directly to the AI API provider (DeepSeek or OpenAI) for processing. We do not store or have access to these problems or the generated solutions.

4. Cookies and Tracking

We do not use any cookies or tracking technologies. The website does not include any analytics or advertising scripts.

5. Third-Party Services

Our application relies on third-party AI services (DeepSeek and OpenAI). We encourage you to review their privacy policies to understand how they handle data.

  • DeepSeek Privacy Policy
  • OpenAI Privacy Policy

6. Data Security

Since we don't store any user data, there is no risk of data breaches from our side. All data processing happens either in your browser or directly with the AI API providers.

7. Children's Privacy

Our service does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal identifiable information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

9. Contact Us
