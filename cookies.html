Cookie Policy

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and provide information to the site owners.

2. How We Use Cookies

MicroSolver AI does not use any cookies. We believe in maintaining your privacy and do not employ any tracking technologies.

3. Third-Party Cookies

Our application does not include any third-party services that set cookies. We do not use analytics, advertising, or other third-party scripts that might place cookies on your device.

4. Local Storage

We use your browser's local storage to save your API keys and preferences. This data remains on your device and is not transmitted to our servers. You can clear this data at any time through your browser settings.

Note: Local storage is different from cookies. It's a browser feature that allows websites to store data locally on your device. This data is only accessible by the website that created it and is not sent to any servers.

5. Your Consent

Since we do not use cookies, we do not require your consent for cookie usage. However, by using our site, you acknowledge that we use local storage as described above.

6. Managing Local Storage

You can manage or clear local storage through your browser settings:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and security → Clear browsing data
  • Firefox: Options → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data
  • Edge: Settings → Privacy, search, and services → Clear browsing data

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Cookie Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Cookie Policy on this page.
